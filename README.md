# Sales-Opportunity-Data-Analysis

## **Project Overview**
This repository contains a **comprehensive data analysis** of sales opportunity data, specifically evaluating the performance between **"New Business"** and **"Upsell"** motions. Using **Python-based data science tools**, the project identifies critical bottlenecks in the sales funnel and provides **strategic recommendations** to improve conversion rates and diversify revenue.

## **Key Insights**
* **The Volume vs. Wins Paradox**: **New Business** generates **4.5x more volume** than Upsells (327 vs. 73 opportunities) but results in **32% fewer absolute wins** (19 vs. 25).
* **Win-Rate Disparity**: **Upsell** motions maintain a healthy **34% win rate**, while **New Business** struggles at an alarmingly low **6%**.
* **Concentration Risk**: Over **93% of revenue** is concentrated in **USD and EUR** currencies, highlighting a significant dependency on North American and European markets.
* **Manager Performance**: Analysis identified **Sales Manager 16 & 18** as top performers in the **Upsell** category, whose methodologies serve as a benchmark for the organization.

## **Tech Stack**
* **Language**: **Python**
* **Libraries**: **Pandas** (Data Manipulation), **NumPy** (Numerical Analysis), **Matplotlib** & **Seaborn** (Data Visualization)
* **Frameworks**: Applied **BANT** (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) for sales qualification analysis.

## **Strategic Recommendations**
1.  **Replicate Upsell Success**: Bridge the win-rate gap by adapting coaching and **knowledge transfer** from high-performing **Upsell** managers to **New Business** teams.
2.  **Stricter Pipeline Hygiene**: Implement a **monthly purge** of "dead deals" (0% probability) to ensure **forecasting accuracy** and focus on viable leads.
3.  **Market Diversification**: Target expansion into **GBP, CAD, and India/Asia** markets to reduce the financial vulnerability caused by **USD/EUR concentration**.
4.  **Enhanced Qualification**: Reduce high **"Qualified Out"** rates in **New Business** by implementing more rigorous entry criteria at the **Opportunity Accepted** stage.
